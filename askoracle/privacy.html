AskOracle Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-25

This Privacy Policy explains how AskOracle collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Spoken Questions and Speech Recognition

AskOracle allows you to ask questions using your device microphone. When you use voice input, the app accesses the microphone and uses speech recognition features to convert your spoken words into text.

We do not use your spoken questions to build advertising profiles. We do not sell your audio or transcriptions.

Question History and Saved Readings

The app may store your recent questions, oracle results, saved readings, theme preferences, and share card style preferences locally on your device so the app can function properly and preserve your experience.

This information is stored on-device unless you explicitly share content yourself.

Shared Content

AskOracle allows you to generate and share oracle cards. These cards are created on your device. If you choose to share them using another app or service, that sharing is handled by the destination you select, and their privacy practices will apply.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock premium features.

All payments are processed securely through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. We may receive limited purchase-related information, such as transaction status, to unlock or restore premium access.

Device and App Functionality Data

The app may use limited device-level information required for core functionality, such as language, operating system version, and app settings. This is used only to operate the app and improve compatibility and usability.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Process spoken questions and convert speech to text
  • Generate and display oracle readings
  • Save reading history and user preferences on-device
  • Enable premium purchases and restore purchases
  • Support app functionality, stability, and compatibility

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information and we do not operate our own advertising network.

Some app features rely on Apple services, such as App Store purchase processing and speech recognition functionality. Information required for those services may be processed by Apple according to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

If you choose to share a generated oracle card through another app or platform, the information you share is handled by the service you select.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple services for speech recognition and in-app purchases.

  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Data Retention

AskOracle is designed to keep reading history and preferences locally on your device, unless you remove the app or clear that data through app functionality or device settings.

Purchase records may be retained by Apple as part of App Store transaction history.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will review the issue.

7. Your Choices

You can control certain permissions and data through your device settings, including:

  • Microphone access
  • Speech recognition access
  • Photo and sharing permissions, if applicable

You can also manage and restore purchases through Apple’s App Store systems.
