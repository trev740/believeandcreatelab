Installation

macOS

  1. Download the macOS .pkg installer.
  2. Open the installer and follow the on-screen steps.
  3. Open or restart OBS Studio.
  4. Add StreamLens Device as a source in OBS.
  5. Open StreamLens on your iPhone. The app should find OBS automatically when both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network.

Windows

  1. Download and unzip the Windows plugin ZIP.
  2. Close OBS Studio completely.
  3. Copy the included data and obs-plugins folders into your OBS Studio folder.
  4. Open OBS Studio and add StreamLens Device as a source.
  5. Open StreamLens on your iPhone. The app should find OBS automatically when both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network.
