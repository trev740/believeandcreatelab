StreamLens Privacy Policy

StreamLens is designed to keep your camera and microphone stream local between your iPhone and your own computer.

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Overview

StreamLens lets you use your iPhone as a wireless camera for OBS Studio and other supported desktop workflows. The app sends video and audio from your iPhone to your own computer over your local Wi-Fi network.

We do not receive, store, collect, sell, or share your video, audio, or personal data.

Data Collection

StreamLens does not collect personal data from the app.

  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use tracking.
  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not upload your camera or microphone stream to our servers.
  • We do not store your video or audio.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data.

Camera and Microphone

StreamLens asks for access to your camera and microphone so it can stream video and audio from your iPhone to your own computer.

Your camera and microphone stream is transmitted locally to your computer on the same Wi-Fi network. StreamLens does not send your video or audio to our servers.

Local Network

StreamLens uses your local network to discover and connect to the StreamLens desktop plugin running on your computer. Your iPhone and computer must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network for the app to work.

Subscriptions and Purchases

StreamLens may offer optional subscriptions through Apple’s in-app purchase system. Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple.

We do not receive your payment card details.

Third-Party Services

StreamLens does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

StreamLens is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not include social features, messaging, public profiles, or user-generated content feeds.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
